Title: HR-Analytics-Dashboard

Description: This HR Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze workforce data and provide actionable insights into employee demographics, attrition, training, work-life balance, and departmental performance. The dashboard helps HR teams monitor key workforce metrics and support data-driven decision-making.

Tools

Key KPIs:
Total Employees: 50,000
Average Attrition Rate: 50%
Total Attrition: 25,105
Total Departments: 6
Average Employee Age: 38.97 Years

Dashboard Insights:
1. Department-wise Working Years Analysis -
Analyzed average employee experience across departments.
Software department shows the highest average working years.
Helps identify workforce stability and retention trends.
2. Work-Life Balance by Job Role -
Compared employee count and average work-life balance across different job roles.
Identified roles with better employee satisfaction and work-life balance scores.
3. Gender Distribution -
Visualized workforce distribution by gender.
Male and female employee counts are nearly balanced, indicating workforce diversity.
4. Department-wise Training Hours -
Analyzed total training hours completed by employees across departments.
Sales department recorded the highest training participation.
Helps evaluate employee development initiatives.
5. Marital Status Analysis -
Examined employee count by marital status categories (Single, Married, Divorced).
Provides demographic insights for HR planning and policy decisions.

Impact:
Enabled quick monitoring of employee performance and HR metrics.
Improved visibility into attrition trends and workforce demographics.
Supported strategic HR decision-making through interactive visualizations.
Simplified analysis of training, diversity, and employee engagement data.

This project strengthened my ability to transform raw HR data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards, helping organizations make informed workforce and talent management decisions.
